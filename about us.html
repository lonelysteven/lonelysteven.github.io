The best choice for building your

S&S Computer Solution established in 1997, aimed to be the best computer hardwares supplier in Malaysia. We provide the best services in supplying multiple brands of hardwares such as CPU, Motherboard, RAM, Cooling Solution, Graphic Cards, PSU, Storages, Cases, and so on. The brands include Intel, AMD, MSI, Asus, Gigabyte, ASRock, Kingston, Apacer, Corsair, Cooler Master, and so on.

We also provide service in building custom PC requested by our customers. We had built over 500 custom PC over the years. Repair service is available as well.
